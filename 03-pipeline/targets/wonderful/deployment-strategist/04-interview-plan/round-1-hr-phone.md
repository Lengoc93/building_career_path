# Round 1 — HR Phone Call (15–30 min)

**Goal of this round (theirs):** screen for signal, motivation, English fluency,
comp realism, logistics, and "startup mindset." Low technical depth.
**Goal of this round (mine):** be a clear yes on fit + energy; anchor my
differentiated positioning; gather intel; don't lock a hard comp number yet.

## The 60-second intro (rehearse verbatim-ish)

> "I'm an AI product leader with 10+ years across AI delivery, fintech, and digital
> platforms in SEA and global markets. The most relevant piece: at Trusting Social I
> took an AI **voice agent** from product thesis to **live US production** for
> enterprise clients in a heavily regulated environment — owning the deployment
> across product, engineering, and the customer, and driving measurable adoption and
> revenue impact after launch. That end-to-end deployment loop is exactly what this
> Deployment Strategist role owns — and I'm a Vietnam/SEA native, which matters given
> you're entering a market you're still exploring. That combination is why I reached
> out."

## Likely questions → my angle

| Question | Angle |
| :------- | :---- |
| "Tell me about yourself." | The 60-sec intro above. End on the VN + deployment combination. |
| "Why Wonderful / why this role?" | Localization thesis + 0→1 greenfield market + I've done the regulated version of this deployment. Optimizing for ownership and frontier, not stability. |
| "Are you comfortable fully remote, English, global team?" | Yes — delivered into US production and did cross-border DD at EY Singapore in English with senior stakeholders across timezones. |
| "What are your comp expectations?" | See `07-comp-and-negotiation.md`. Acknowledge VN band isn't set; reference the APAC ~VND 3–4B benchmark she gave; say I want to understand equity structure before anchoring. **Do not name a hard number first.** |
| "When could you start / notice period?" | [FILL IN — current notice at Vingroup]. |
| "What do you know about us?" | 30-sec version of `01-company-research.md`: Seed→Series B in ~a year, $2B, localization moat, 30+ countries, APAC entry. |
| "Are you a builder / startup type?" | Yes — ~9,500-line state machine I owned, from-zero 6-market pipeline, thesis-to-production. I build, not just coordinate. |

## Questions I ask (pick 2–3 — see `06-questions-to-ask.md`)

- "How mature is the VN pipeline — any signed pilots, or fully pre-market?"
- "For the ~6 months before the legal entity is ready, what's the employment vehicle?"
- "Who would I report to day-to-day before a VN GM is in place?"

## Do / Don't

- **Do** name the VN-market-intelligence advantage — it's my differentiator and she
  already told me it's their gap.
- **Do** keep energy high and concise; this is a vibe + clarity screen.
- **Don't** commit to a hard salary number.
- **Don't** over-explain fintech/voice as if it's a limitation — frame as depth.
