# Round 5 — Meeting with the General Manager

**Altitude shift: commercial + market + culture.** The GM owns the VN market's success
and P&L thinking. Less "how do you deploy," more "will this person help us *win
Vietnam*, navigate Tier-1 enterprise buyers, and thrive in our culture." This is where
my **VN/SEA market intelligence** becomes the headline, not the footnote.

> Note: per the recruiter, a VN GM may not be in place yet — this round could be a
> regional GM. Either way, pitch to the commercial/market owner.

## Lead with the moat

Deployment competence got me through the CTO round; **market intelligence closes the
GM.** They've admitted they don't understand VN yet. I do.

- **VN enterprise buyer behavior:** how FMCG, retail, aviation, telecom, and tech buy
  in VN — procurement cycles, who signs, pilot-to-contract dynamics, the trust barrier
  for a foreign AI vendor, data-residency/regulatory sensitivities.
- **Localization reality:** where a translated agent fails in Vietnamese (register,
  politeness hierarchy, code-switching, regional dialect) — this is Wonderful's whole
  thesis and I can make it concrete for VN.
- **Go-to-market texture (without being sales):** which logos and verticals to
  sequence first for fast, referenceable wins; where the landmines are.

## Talking points for the GM

- **Land-and-expand thinking:** first deployment is a beachhead; I mine production data
  for the next use case and turn one Tier-1 logo into a reference that unlocks the
  vertical. Frame deployment as the engine of market credibility.
- **Stakeholder navigation:** my 6-market pipeline (AU/PH/IN/VN/US) each had distinct
  regulatory/operational/data requirements; I authored 80% of the DD/proposal materials.
  I navigate multi-entity, multi-stakeholder complexity — the GM's daily reality.
- **Impact-and-adoption mindset:** my KPIs have always been business outcomes
  (conversion, revenue, adoption), not activity — aligned with this role's KPI set.
- **Startup-mindset proof:** 0→1 from thesis to production; from-zero pipeline; I build
  playbooks where none exist. I'm energized by the greenfield, not nervous about it.

## Questions to ask the GM (see `06-questions-to-ask.md`)
