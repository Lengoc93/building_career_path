# Questions to Ask — by stakeholder

Good questions do double duty: they surface intel *and* signal seniority. Pick 2–3 per
round; don't interrogate. Prioritized within each list.

## HR / Recruiter (Round 1)
- How mature is the VN pipeline — any signed pilots/LOIs, or fully pre-market?
- For the ~6 months before the legal entity is ready, what's the employment vehicle
  (contractor, EOR, regional entity)?
- Who would I report to day-to-day before a VN GM is in place?
- What does the equity component look like — and how is it structured pre-VN-entity?
- What's the target timeline to fill this role, and where are you in the process?

## Case Study round (Round 2)
- When you enter a new-language market, what usually breaks first in a deployment —
  integration, agent quality, or customer-side adoption?
- What does a "successful" first enterprise deployment look like to Wonderful — which
  metric matters most: containment, CSAT, or expansion?
- How standardized is the deployment playbook today vs. reinvented per customer?

## Technical round (Round 3)
- How do you evaluate agent quality across languages today — golden sets, LLM-judge,
  human review? What's the weakest link?
- Where does per-market customization live — prompts/context, retrieval, fine-tuning,
  or config? (Gauges how localization actually works.)
- What's the human-in-the-loop / escalation model for low-confidence turns in production?

## CTO (Round 4)
- How do you keep localization per market from fragmenting into N bespoke forks of the
  platform?
- Where does the Deployment Strategist's authority end and Product's begin on roadmap
  decisions driven by customer needs?
- What breaks first — technically — when you enter a brand-new-language market like VN?
- Pre-GM, how much latitude does this role have to define the local deployment playbook?

## General Manager (Round 5)
- What does winning VN in the first 12 months look like — logos, revenue, references?
- How are you sequencing verticals for VN entry (FMCG vs. telecom vs. aviation…)?
- What worries you most about entering Vietnam, and how could this role de-risk it?
- How much of the VN playbook do you expect the first hires to define vs. inherit from
  global?

## Cross-cutting (ask whoever is right)
- What's the story behind the GM/CTO/Strategist/FDE/PO/GTM pod — who's already hired for
  VN, and who am I building alongside?
- 12 months out, how will you know this hire was a great decision?
- What's the biggest reason a deployment has failed or stalled for Wonderful so far?
  (Shows I care about real failure modes, not the happy path.)
