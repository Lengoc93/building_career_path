## Role Overview
We are looking for a Deployment Strategist to lead the successful implementation of
AI-powered solutions for enterprise customers. In this role, you will bridge the gap
between technology, execution, and customer success—ensuring AI initiatives are
translated into tangible business outcomes.

You will manage complex deployments from initial discovery and planning through
production launch, collaborating closely with customers, Product, and Engineering
teams. The ideal candidate thrives in fast-paced environments, enjoys solving complex
challenges, and is passionate about delivering impactful technology solutions.

## Job Description (responsibilities)
- Lead the end-to-end deployment of AI solutions and enterprise platforms for customers.
- Scope requirements, success metrics, and integration plans for Pilots and onboardings.
- Coordinate platform implementation, integrations, and agent refinement toward production launches.
- Align Product, Engineering, and customer teams on deliverables, timelines, and SLAs.
- Monitor AI agent performance in production and drive follow-up improvements.
- Identify and develop new AI use cases with customers to expand adoption and impact.
- Track progress, manage risks, and remove blockers throughout the delivery lifecycle.

## Requirements
- 3+ years of experience in technical problem-solving roles.
- Comfort working with technical concepts such as **APIs, data flows, and system integrations**.
- Ability to manage multiple stakeholders and keep complex efforts moving forward.
- **High-agency mindset:** you don't wait for perfect requirements and are comfortable
  creating clarity, proposing direction, and driving decisions.
- Proactive, hands-on mindset and interest in AI-driven products.
- Strong communication skills, project management, organization.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related technical field or equivalent experience.

## Nice to have
- Engineering background or prior hands-on technical experience.
- Background in **management consulting, strategy consulting, or technical consulting**,

  with senior stakeholders.
- Experience working in a **high-growth or early-stage startup** environment.
