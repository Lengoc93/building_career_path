## My questions to HR

1. How is the benefit package structured and paid?
2. Who are the direct stakeholders of this role and the level of dependency?
3. What are the key direct responsible KPIs of this role?
4. Is this open as a management role or expert only?

> compensation is expected to be around **VND 3–4B, including base salary and
> equity**."

**On customers:**
> "They're aiming to work closely with **Tier-1 enterprise customers** across
> industries like **FMCG, retail, aviation, telecommunications, technology**…."

**On reporting & role shape:**
> "This role is **under the CTO** and they're looking for someone who can **hybrid
> between strategy consulting, product management, and project management**."
> "And **you won't need to do sales**."

**On KPIs:**
> "Performance will be measured by the **successful delivery and adoption of AI
> solutions, customer satisfaction, project execution, and the overall impact
> delivered to enterprise clients**."

**On level:**
> "I think this position is **more like an expert**, but it comes with a **high level
> of ownership and influence** because you'll **lead the customer and work across
> multiple teams**."
