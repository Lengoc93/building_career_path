# Opportunity Pipeline Tracker

The single view of every live opportunity. One row per target. Keep `Next action`
and `Owner`/date current — this is the roadmap you'd demand of any PM.

**Stages:** `Sourced` → `Researching` → `Applied/Intro` → `Interviewing` →
`Offer` → `Closed (won/lost)`

| Target | Role | Market | Stage | Next action | Due | Folder |
| :----- | :--- | :----- | :---- | :---------- | :-- | :----- |
| Tictag | CTO-track / AI Product | — | Interviewing | Review roleplay prep before next round | — | `targets/tictag/` |

## Notes

- Add a new target by copying the `targets/tictag/` folder pattern
  (`proposal/`, `prep/`, `notes/`) and adding a row above.
- Move won/lost opportunities to the bottom rather than deleting — the history is
  useful signal on what markets and framings convert.
